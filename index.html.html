About Us

Welcome to Naga Homestay, your home away from home in Kudus. We are a family-run accommodation offering a warm, friendly atmosphere, clean rooms, and peaceful surroundings. Just 4 minutes’ drive to Alun-Alun and 3 minutes to Menara Kudus.
